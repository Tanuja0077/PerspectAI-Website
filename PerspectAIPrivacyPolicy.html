At perspectAI, we provide a series of Virtual Reality, web and mobile games, puzzles, and challenges (collectively “Games”) through which we develop your perspectAI User Profile. We may collect information about you from a variety of sources, including information we collect from you directly when you log onto our Application or use our Games.

What information do we collect directly from you?

To use our Application, Users must sign-up or log-in into our application with an email address. Users can access our Games on our website, or download them from the App Store.

Account information

We will ask you to create a perspectAI account and password the first time you use our service.

We use cookies and other tracking technologies to automatically collect information about you when you visit our Application or use our Services, including IP address, browser type, operating system, date and time of visit, time spent on and/or logged in to Application, pages visited, links clicked, and other activity. We may use such information with the information we have collected from users.
